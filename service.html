Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: How can I enroll in Quran classes?
  • A: You can sign up online or visit the mosque to speak to our coordinator and register for a class.
  • Q: Do you offer any youth programs?
  • A: Yes! We organize monthly meetups and events aimed at empowering and connecting the youth with their faith.
  • Q: How can I donate to the mosque?
  • A: Donations can be made online through our donation portal or by visiting the mosque directly.
  • Q: Are there any special events for Eid?
  • A: Absolutely! We host an Eid celebration every year with prayers, food, and community activities. Stay updated on our events page.
