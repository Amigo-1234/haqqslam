About Haqslam Mosque

Our Mission

At Haqslam Mosque, our mission is to create a nurturing environment where faith, community, and spirituality flourish. We aim to provide a space for worship, education, and unity among all Muslims while promoting values of peace and understanding.

Our History

Established in 2004, Haqslam Mosque has been a cornerstone of the local Muslim community. Over the years, we have expanded our services to include Quranic studies, youth programs, and charity initiatives, all while maintaining our dedication to fostering faith and connection.

What We Offer

We offer daily prayers, educational programs, and community events designed to bring people together. Whether you're seeking a place for worship, learning, or community, Haqslam Mosque is here to serve you.
